The Problem

Manufacturing is Broken
at the Finish Line

You deliver the part. Then you wait. The payment process hasn't changed in 40 years, and it's costing small manufacturers their business.

01
⏳
Payment Delays Kill Cash Flow

Net-60 and Net-90 terms force manufacturers to self-finance production. You bear 100% of the risk while buyers sit on your working capital.

60–90 daysaverage DSO in custom manufacturing
02
⚖️
Disputes Have No Referee

When a buyer claims a part was "not to spec," there's no objective record. Disputes drag on for weeks, payment gets withheld, and relationships collapse.

~15%of MTO orders end in payment disputes
03
📉
Idle Capacity Goes Monetised

Small manufacturers can't sell unused production slots. There's no marketplace. That capacity evaporates: revenue that could have existed, doesn't.

30–40%average idle capacity in US 3D print bureaus
The Solution

Smart Escrow.
Instant Settlement.

OnChainMake wraps every custom manufacturing order in a smart contract that holds, verifies, and releases payment automatically. No banks, no intermediaries, no delays.

1
Buyer Deposits into Escrow

The buyer locks USDC into a smart contract before production begins. The manufacturer sees confirmed funds. Zero credit risk, zero chasing invoices.

2
Milestones Trigger Verification

As production hits key milestones, the smart contract checks objective criteria. No human gatekeeping. No subjective disputes.

3
Funds Released in Seconds

Upon milestone completion, payment flows directly to the manufacturer. On Arbitrum blockchain. In under 60 seconds. Always.

4
Capacity Tokenised & Tradeable

Unused production slots become tokenised assets (CSTs) tradeable in a marketplace. Your idle time becomes revenue.
