How It Works for Buyers

Browse, Lock,
Track, Approve.

You stay in control at every stage. Funds never leave escrow until each milestone is independently verified on-chain.

1
Browse Verified Manufacturers

Search a curated network of vetted makers by technology, location, and capacity. Request quotes, agree milestone terms, and confirm scope — all before a cent moves.

2
Lock Funds into Escrow

Deposit USDC into an audited smart contract on Arbitrum. Your funds sit untouched — not held by OnChainMake, not accessible to the maker — until milestones are met.

3
Track Production in Real Time

Every milestone is logged on-chain and visible on your dashboard. A tamper-proof, timestamped record at every step — no chasing suppliers for updates.

4
Delivery Confirmed. Settlement Automatic.

Final delivery triggers automatic payment release. If any milestone falls short, the smart contract enforces the agreed terms. You keep your funds or receive a proportional release — never exposed.

🔒 How Escrow Protects You
🏛️
Non-Custodial

Your USDC is locked in audited contract code. Neither OnChainMake nor the manufacturer can access it — only verified milestone completion unlocks each tranche.

📐
Milestone-Gated Release

You define the milestones and payment split upfront. Each tranche releases independently when that milestone is verified — no bulk releases, no surprises.

⛓️
Immutable Audit Trail

Every deposit, verification, and release is logged permanently on Arbitrum. Your compliance team gets an exportable, tamper-proof record for every order.

⚖️
Automatic Dispute Resolution

If a milestone is missed or disputed, the contract enforces the agreed terms without arbitration — objective, fast, and final.
