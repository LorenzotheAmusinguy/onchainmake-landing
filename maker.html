How It Works

Four Steps.
Zero Chasing.

OnChainMake replaces invoices, follow-up emails, and payment disputes with a smart contract that does it all automatically.

1
Buyer Locks Funds Before You Start

Your buyer deposits USDC into a smart contract escrow before production begins. You see the funds confirmed on your dashboard. Reduced payment risk. Zero chasing invoices.

2
You Set Your Production Milestones

Break the order into milestones — print complete, QA passed, delivery confirmed. Each milestone has a payment amount attached. You define the terms upfront.

3
Hit the Milestone. Get Paid.

When you mark a milestone complete, the smart contract releases that payment directly to your wallet. On Arbitrum. In under 60 seconds. No approval required from the buyer.

4
Tokenise Unused Capacity

Got spare production slots? Tokenise them as Capacity Slot Tokens (CSTs) and list them on the OnChainMake marketplace. Your idle time becomes a revenue stream.
